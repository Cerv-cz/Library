About Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game set in a vast open world known as the Lands Between. Developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, it was created in collaboration with fantasy novelist George R.R. Martin, who crafted the mythos underlying the game's universe. Players take on the role of the Tarnished — exiles called back to the Lands Between after the shattering of the Elden Ring.

The game builds upon the beloved Souls formula established by FromSoftware's previous titles — Dark Souls, Bloodborne, and Sekiro — while introducing unprecedented scope and freedom. For the first time in the studio's history, players can explore a fully open world filled with dungeons, castles, hidden secrets, and formidable demigod bosses. Horseback traversal via spectral steed Torrent further expands exploration possibilities.

The seamless blend of rich lore, deeply strategic combat, and a breathtaking world resulted in near-universal critical acclaim. It won Game of the Year at The Game Awards 2022 and shipped over 20 million copies, becoming FromSoftware's most commercially successful title to date.

Key Features
  • Vast Open World: Explore the Lands Between — a sweeping open world with six major regions, each featuring unique biomes, dungeons, catacombs, and ruins to discover.
  • Deep Combat System: Master a rich arsenal of weapons, sorceries, incantations, and Ashes of War. Every encounter demands strategy, patience, and skill.
  • George R.R. Martin's Mythos: The world's history and lore were developed by the author of A Song of Ice and Fire, lending unprecedented narrative depth to the setting.
  • Multiplayer & Co-op: Summon other players into your world for cooperative play or engage in PvP invasions for intense online moments.
  • Build Customization: Choose from hundreds of weapons, armor sets, and magic abilities to craft a completely unique Tarnished with your preferred playstyle.
  • Shadow of the Erdtree DLC: The massive expansion released in 2024 introduces an entirely new region — the Land of Shadow — with new bosses, weapons, and narrative revelations.

Combat & Mechanics

Elden Ring's combat is the culmination of FromSoftware's decade-long refinement of action RPG mechanics. Players must carefully manage stamina, read enemy attack patterns, and leverage their build's strengths. The introduction of Ashes of War — special abilities that can be applied to weapons — dramatically expands combat versatility.

Stealth mechanics allow players to approach some encounters silently, setting up backstab criticals. The Spirit Ashes system lets players summon spectral allies during combat, providing welcome support in difficult encounters. Horseback combat on Torrent opens up entirely new tactical possibilities in the open world.

Narrative Overview
The Shattering
Long ago, the Greater Will sent a golden order to the Lands Between through the Elden Ring, granting grace and power to all under its light. But Queen Marika shattered the Elden Ring, plunging the world into conflict as her demigod children fought over its shards — the Great Runes.
The Tarnished Returns
The Tarnished — once exiled from the Lands Between after losing grace — are called back by the guidance of a golden light. You are one such Tarnished, tasked with collecting the Great Runes from the demigods and reforging the Elden Ring to become the Elden Lord.
The Demigods and Their Domains
Six demigod rulers hold sway over the major regions of the Lands Between, each corrupted or broken by the Shattering. From Godrick the Grafted in Stormveil Castle to Malenia, Blade of Miquella in the rot-soaked Haligtree, each presents a formidable challenge and carries a fragment of the Ring.
Multiple Endings
The Elden Ring features multiple branching endings determined by player choices and NPC questlines throughout the game — from the Age of Fracture to the radical Age of Stars, each reflecting different philosophies for the future of the Lands Between.
